My name is **Sang woo Ham** (you can call me, Sang). I am currently Ph.D. candidate in civil engineering, Purdue university, West Lafayatte, IN, working with Prof. Panagiota Karava.

My goal is to be **a responsible, polymathic, and a practical engineer**. The completion of tasks under trust and pursuing sustainability for the Earth are my top responsibility/priority. Learning/applying new things give me what to do when I confront challenges. Based on the responsibility and experience, I would like to bring the research into real world practice by connecting multiple stakeholders such as researchers, engineers, developers, etc.

I am interested in **Building energy analysis/modeling by using R/Python through various Probabilistic models with Pyro/Stan/Pymc3**. My current research is mostly related to online energy models via Bayesian framework and also worked for several building energy application such as data center cooling, CFD, EnergyPlus, co-simulation, heat exchanger design, and various field experiment like environmental chamber design, smart home interface design/installation/data collection/experiment.
